Membership Options

Diamond Health Club offers a variety of membership programs, including one sure to meet your specific needs and budget. Pick up a membership form at the club or e-mail Ron Cill to request one.

Individual memberships

You must be eighteen or older to qualify for an individual membership.

  • Gold: Full access to the club and access to special Gold Member areas.
  • Full: Full access to the club during all open hours.
  • Weekdays: A discount program, allowing access to the club from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m., Monday through Friday.
  • Evening: A discount program, allowing access from 5 p.m. to 10 p.m. every day of the week.
  • Early Bird: A discount program, allowing access to the club from 5 a.m. to 9 a.m. every day of the week.
  • Weekend: Enjoy the club on Saturdays and Sundays. Ideal for weekend warriors.
  • Seniors: We offer special membership rates for individual seniors.

Family memberships

  • With child care: This option is best for families who plan to use the children’s play room, which is available for children ages 6 weeks to 12 years. The play room is open every day. Children under 6 can have Story time in a nearby room while family work out in the play room.
  • No child care: Same as the family option but without access to the play room.
  • Seniors: We provide special membership rates for senior couples. Pick up a membership form for specific rules regarding eligibility.

Temporary memberships

  • Monthly: For our longer-term visitors this membership provides access to the club for one month.
  • Weekly: For our short-term visitors this membership provides access to the club for one week.
  • Daily: A card that provides full access to the Diamond Health Club (except child care facilities) for a specific number of full days. Punch cards make great gifts and are available for 5, 10, and 20 visits.
